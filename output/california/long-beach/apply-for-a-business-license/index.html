Eligibility

Any individual or entity conducting business within Long Beach city limits must obtain a business license, including home-based businesses, contractors, and out-of-city businesses operating locally.

Required Documents

Completed business license application form
Valid government-issued ID
Seller’s permit (if applicable)
Fictitious business name statement (if applicable)
Proof of zoning compliance (if applicable)
Federal Employer Identification Number (FEIN) or Social Security Number

How to Apply

1

Determine your business classification and applicable fees. Complete the online business license application form. Submit required documents and pay the applicable fees. Receive confirmation and your business license certificate. Renew your business license annually before the expiration date.

Important Deadline

Business licenses must be renewed annually by the expiration date to avoid penalties.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Failing to renew the license annually, incomplete application forms, missing required documents, not paying applicable fees, misunderstanding fee structure based on business type and size.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who needs a business license in Long Beach?

Any individual or entity conducting business within Long Beach city limits, including home-based and out-of-city businesses operating locally.

How much does a business license cost?

Fees start at $45 and vary based on business type, number of employees, and gross receipts.

How long does it take to process the application?

Typically 5-7 business days after submission and payment.
