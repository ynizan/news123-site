Making Government Permits Accessible to Everyone

We're building the most comprehensive database of government permits, licenses, and transactions across the United States.

The Problem We're Solving

Navigating government permits and licenses is frustratingly difficult. Information is scattered across thousands of government websites, forms are confusing, requirements are unclear, and costs are hidden. Business owners, homeowners, and individuals waste countless hours trying to figure out what permits they need and how to get them.

PermitIndex changes that. We aggregate permit information from across all 50 states, making it searchable, comparable, and understandable in one place.

Our Mission

To create universal access to government transaction information. Every permit, license, and government service should be easy to find, understand, and complete. We believe transparency in government processes benefits everyoneâ€”individuals, businesses, and communities.
